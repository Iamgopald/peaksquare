PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: January 2025

At PeakSquare Estates, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out contact forms or subscribe to our market updates. This includes:

  • Name
  • Phone Number & Email Address
  • Property Preferences (Location, Budget, Configuration)

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used strictly for professional purposes to deliver the services you requested:

  • To contact you regarding specific properties you inquired about.
  • To schedule private site visits or consultations.
  • To verify your identity as a genuine buyer or seller to maintain the exclusivity of our network.

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to ensure your personal details are safe from unauthorized access. We strictly do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to third-party marketing agencies.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or wish to update your preferences, please contact us directly via the contact form on our homepage.
