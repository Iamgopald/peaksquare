Privacy Policy

At PeakSquare Estates, we manage luxury real estate with the highest discretion. Your privacy is paramount. This policy details our commitment to your data security and confidentiality.

1. Data Acquisition

We collect information necessary to curate your property search, including contact details (name, email, phone number) and investment preferences shared via our private access forms, search queries, or direct consultations.

2. Professional Use

Your data is used exclusively to facilitate private viewings, verify listing access, and provide high-value market insights specifically tailored to your interest in the Pune luxury market. We do not sell or trade your personal information to third-party marketers.

3. Digital Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal information from unauthorized access. Our digital environment is monitored to ensure the integrity of our private inventory and client database.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic via Google Analytics. This helps us understand which luxury collections are most sought after, allowing us to better curate our portfolio.
